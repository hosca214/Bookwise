# Landing Page Hero Clarity — Design Spec
Date: 2026-04-30

## Problem
The current hero headline ("Keep more of what you earn.") and subhead ("The financial clarity tool built for wellness coaches.") do not clearly communicate what Bookwise is or does. User feedback: one person asked if it was a budgeting app, another said "I don't get it."

## Approved Design

### What changes
Only the hero section copy in `app/landing/page.tsx`. No structural changes. No other sections touched.

### New copy

**Eyebrow badge:**
Financial clarity for coaches, trainers, and bodyworkers

**Headline:**
Always know where your money goes. Never get surprised by your tax bill.

**Subhead (three short lines + closer):**
Track every dollar in and out.
See exactly what to save for taxes.
Know what to pay yourself this month.
Your money finally makes sense. Clear numbers, calm mind.

**CTAs (unchanged):**
- Primary: "Start free" (links to /login)
- Secondary: "See how it works" (links to #how-it-works)
- Trust line: "Free during early access. No credit card required."

### Design decisions
- Eyebrow names the audience explicitly so the headline can focus on pain points (borrowed from SilverIntelligence.ai clarity pattern)
- Three short lines replace a paragraph — scannable, not a wall of text
- The closer ("Your money finally makes sense. Clear numbers, calm mind.") blends directness with the Zen Bookkeeper brand voice
- No em dashes anywhere (CLAUDE.md rule)
- Copy is honest: the app tracks and shows — it does not transfer money or set anything aside on the user's behalf

### What stays the same
Everything else on the page: nervous system section, stats, pull quote, how it works, features, who it's for, origin story, FAQ, waitlist CTA, footer.
